O Advanced Partner Summit

It is with great excitement that Genesys EMEA in partnership with ... (gold sponsor?) announces the Genesys EMEA PureCloud Advanced Partner Summit combined with the very first EMEA PureCloud Partners Hackathon. The Advanced Partner Summit will start on the 23rd of October while the Hackathon will start a day earlier.

Entries are open to all Genesys PureCloud partners and must be made through our registration form.

Challenge: What is the challenge for this event?

Early Access: You will get early access to some beta features. These features will be enabled for your org shortly before the hackathon (hopefully, the chat API everyone has been so eager to play with!).

Access to Dev: We will also bring 1 or 2 PureCloud developers from the US to answer some of your questions and present some of the features coming out soon!
